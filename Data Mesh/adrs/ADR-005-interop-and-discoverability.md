# ADR-005: Prioritize interoperability and discoverability via a catalog

## Status
Accepted

## Date
2026-01-11

## Context
A mesh fails if products cannot be found, understood, or safely joined across domains.

## Decision
Require publishing to a catalog with:
- searchable metadata (owner, domain, contract version)
- lineage and usage metrics
- standardized metrics definitions where applicable (semantic layer)

## Consequences
- higher reuse and fewer duplicate datasets
- requires catalog adoption and consistent metadata hygiene
