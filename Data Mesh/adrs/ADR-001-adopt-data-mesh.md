# ADR-001: Adopt Data Mesh operating model for analytics scaling

## Status
Accepted

## Date
2026-01-11

## Context
Centralized data engineering cannot scale to meet domain demand. Ownership and semantics are closest to domain teams.

## Decision
Adopt a **Data Mesh** model:
- domains own data products end-to-end
- platform provides paved roads
- governance defines standards and automates enforcement

## Consequences
- improved scalability and accountability
- requires strong platform team and adoption enablement
