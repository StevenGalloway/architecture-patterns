# Data Product Standard (Template)

## Required
- **Owner**: team + contact (on-call) + escalation
- **Contract**: schema, semantics, SLOs (freshness, availability)
- **Classification**: PII fields tagged; retention and access policy
- **Quality**: minimum test suite and acceptable thresholds
- **Lineage**: upstream sources and downstream consumers (where known)
- **Versioning**: semver; breaking change policy; changelog

## Recommended
- Cost/usage budgets and chargeback tags
- Data observability: anomalies, drift, freshness monitors
- Consumer guides: common joins, example queries, known caveats
